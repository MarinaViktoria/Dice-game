Rules of the dice game

User 1 rolls the dice. If the number is everything but not 1, it will be added to his current score. User rolls the dice again... and again... until he decides to hold the number. In this case his current score will be added to his total score.
Or he gets 1. In this case nothing will be added to his total score and another player gets his chance to play. A player, who gets 100 points wins! You can start one more time and reset the game.
